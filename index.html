If your favorite shoes are looking worse for wear, bring them to our team

Cobblers’ is a trusted name in Lagos for professional shoe repairs. We take the time to restore your shoes original look and feel, giving you many more years of enjoyment.

With years of experience in working on shoes, and a fully equipped workshop, we can repair a wide range of issues, from split soles to leather repairs and lace replacement. We'll aim to have your shoes looking as good as new in no time!

Collections & Deliveries

For your convenience we will collect and deliver your shoe, leather & bag for repairs, your laundry and dry cleaning for cleaning as well as garment repairs.
